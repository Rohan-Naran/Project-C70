Campus Life

school church

was established in 1978

Our school's church has been a vital part of our school life for over 40 years. The students and teachers visit it every morning before the lessons start.



school library

was established in 1980

The school library is probably the most popular place in the school. It has a range of different books, from fantasy to factual, and sometimes students come to the library when they want to go somewhere peaceful.



school auditorium

was established in 1982

When the school auditorium was created, the students and staff at the school were absolutely awed. With amazing lighting and seats with top-class views, the auditorium revolutionized the school. Now, school assemblies are given here, and productions are also put on in the auditorium.

School Achievemts

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

Our debate team won the inter school debate on environment pollution, and are now the current national debate champions. They also won medals which were awarded to them by Sir David Attenborough!

Inter School Football Competition
came second in inter school football competition

Our school's main team for football pushed the limits and came second in the inter school football competition!

Inter School Science Project Competition
won second prize in inter school science project competition

Our school's science team did superbly in this competition, with a unique idea on how to stop animals from becoming extinct
